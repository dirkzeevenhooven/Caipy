Must See

The Highlights

Six iconic experiences that define Cape Town. Don't leave without them.

Icon

Table Mountain

The flat-topped icon of Cape Town — ride the rotating cable car to the summit for 360° views across the peninsula, city and both oceans. One of the New Seven Wonders of Nature.

Book Tickets
Wildlife

Boulders Beach Penguins

Walk alongside a colony of African penguins at this sheltered cove in Simon's Town. The boardwalks take you within metres of these charming birds in their natural habitat.

Plan Visit
Nature

Cape Point

At the dramatic southern tip of the peninsula, dramatic cliffs meet crashing Atlantic waves. Part of the Table Mountain National Park — home to baboons, ostriches and spectacular hiking trails.

Explore
Scenic Drive

Chapman's Peak Drive

One of the world's most spectacular coastal roads — 9km of hairpin bends cut into the cliffs above the Atlantic Ocean. Best at golden hour with the windows down.

Route Info
Culture

Bo-Kaap

Cape Town's most colourful neighbourhood — cobbled streets lined with pastel-painted houses, the oldest mosque in South Africa, and the warm heritage of the Cape Malay community.

Walking Tour
History

Robben Island

Where Nelson Mandela was held prisoner for 18 of his 27 years. A powerful UNESCO World Heritage site — guided by former political prisoners, this is one of the most moving experiences in Africa.

Be Prepared

Everything You Need to Know

Practical advice from someone who's lived in Cape Town for over 10 years.

💳
Money & Cards
Use Revolut for great exchange rates. Notify your bank before travel. Some Dutch cards are not accepted in South Africa. Always carry a credit card as backup.
📱
Stay Connected
Get an Airalo eSIM before you depart — cheap and easy. Good 4G coverage throughout the city. Download an offline Maps.ME map of Cape Town for backup navigation.
🚗
Getting Around
Uber is safe, reliable and very affordable. Rent a car for Peninsula day trips and the Winelands. Avoid metered taxis — they are unreliable and often overpriced.
🛡️
Safety
Travel smart. Leave flashy jewellery at home. Keep your phone in your bag in busy areas. Lock car doors when driving. Use hotel parking or secured underground parking.
💊
Health & Packing
Bring an international power adapter (Type N or M). Pack a portable charger. Strong sunscreen is essential — the African sun is intense. Bring light layers for cool evenings.
🌤️
Weather
Summer runs November to March with averages of 25–28°C. Table Mountain's iconic cloud "tablecloth" can roll in fast — always check conditions before hiking.
💰
Budget Tips
Currency is ZAR (Rand). €1 ≈ R20–22, making Cape Town excellent value for Europeans. Wine from R80/bottle. Uber rides from R50. Excellent restaurants from R180/main.
✈️
Before You Fly
EU citizens receive 90 days visa-free. Travel insurance is essential. Notify your bank. Make sure your passport has at least 30 days validity beyond your return date.
