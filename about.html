The person behind it

Hi, I'm Dirk.
Cape Town is home.

I moved from the Netherlands to Cape Town over ten years ago. What was supposed to be a year turned into a decade — because once this city gets under your skin, leaving stops making sense.

I've hiked every trail on Table Mountain, eaten at every restaurant worth going to, and watched the Atlantic from Clifton more sunsets than I can count. This guide is what I tell every friend who asks: "What should I do in Cape Town?"

"Most travel guides are written by people who visited for a week, or by algorithms that have never visited at all. I wanted to build something different — something that feels like getting advice from a friend who actually lives there."

The Cape Town Guide started as notes I kept for friends visiting from Europe. Over the years those notes grew into something more — a proper guide that covered not just the obvious sights, but the specific restaurants worth booking weeks ahead, the beaches that only locals go to, and the safety knowledge that makes the difference between a great trip and a stressful one.

In 2026 I built Caipy — an AI travel guide trained on everything I know about Cape Town. The idea is simple: you get the same personalised advice I'd give a close friend, instantly, in a conversation. Not a generic itinerary, not a list of sponsored hotels. A real plan, built around you.

Planet Uncharted is the brand — a name that reflects how I think about travel. The best places are always slightly off the map. The best experiences are never in the guidebook. That's what I'm here to help you find.
