Weather Today

Cape Town's weather is notoriously changeable — four seasons in one day is not a cliché here. Check before you go, especially for hikes and beach days.

💨
Cape Doctor alert — strong SE wind today

The Cape Doctor (south-easterly wind) is blowing today. Atlantic-facing beaches like Camps Bay and Clifton will be windy — head to False Bay (Muizenberg, Fish Hoek) for calmer conditions. It's a natural air-conditioning system — the city will be fresh and clear.

🏘️ Hermanus
The whale capital of the world. Whales come within metres of the cliff path. 1.5h from Cape Town. September is peak — book ahead.
⛪ False Bay coastline
From Gordon's Bay to Simon's Town — you can watch from the road or beach. Free, no booking required, spectacular.
🚤 Boat trips
Hermanus and Simon's Town both offer permitted whale-watching boats. 2–3 hours. Book via Dyer Island Cruises or Shark Explorers.
🌊 Boulders Beach
Spot whales from the rocks while watching the penguins. In peak season (Sept–Oct), sightings are almost guaranteed from the shore.
