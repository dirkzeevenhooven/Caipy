Where you stay changes everything. Sea Point puts you on the promenade. Gardens puts you in the city's soul. Camps Bay puts the ocean at your feet. Choose your base first — the rest follows.
Before You Book

Cape Town has four distinct bases — each gives you a completely different experience.

Sea Point is the Atlantic Seaboard's most liveable neighbourhood — great restaurants, a long promenade, and 10 minutes from everywhere. The City Bowl and Gardens are walkable, historic, and full of character. Camps Bay is pure glamour — beach, mountain backdrop, sundowners. The Waterfront is world-class and convenient but can feel touristy. All four are safe, well-serviced, and within easy reach of each other.

Sea Point is the Atlantic Seaboard's most liveable neighbourhood — beach promenade, excellent restaurants, and 10 minutes from the City Bowl. The best base for most first-time visitors.
