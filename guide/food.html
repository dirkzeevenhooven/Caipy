Neighbourhood Gem · Tamboerskloof

The Black Sheep

📍 Tamboerskloof 💰 R400–600 pp ⏱ Book 3+ days ahead 🌟 Locals' favourite
Dirk's Take
"Locals' favourite, zero tourists, exceptional food. Wednesday night is their special menu — it's legendary. Book at least three days ahead or you won't get in."

Tucked into a quiet Tamboerskloof street, The Black Sheep is what every neighbourhood restaurant aspires to be. The menu changes regularly, the wine list is brilliant and fairly priced, and the Wednesday night special menu has a genuine cult following among Cape Town food lovers.

Insider Tips
→ Book Wednesday for their special menu — the best value fine dining in the city
→ Small restaurant — intimate atmosphere, not for large groups over 4
→ The wine list is exceptional — ask for the sommelier's recommendation
→ No tourists. You'll be surrounded entirely by locals — that's the point
