Common questions

Frequently asked.

How does the Caipy itinerary work? +
You chat with Caipy for free — it asks you a few questions about your trip (how long, who with, interests, budget). Once it has everything it needs, you pay €49,- and receive a personalised day-by-day itinerary by email within minutes.
Can I get changes made to my itinerary after I receive it? +
Yes — just email us at info@capetownguide.com with what you'd like adjusted. We'll update it for you. We want your trip to be perfect, not just the itinerary to be delivered.
Is payment secure? +
All payments are processed by Stripe — the same payment infrastructure used by Amazon, Google and Shopify. We never see or store your card details.
Is the guide really based on local knowledge, not AI-generated content? +
Yes. Caipy is an AI built on Dirk's personal knowledge of Cape Town — the restaurants he actually eats at, the hikes he's done, the safety advice he gives friends visiting from Europe. It's not scraped from the internet or trained on generic travel content.
What if I have a question that's not in the itinerary? +
Email us. If it's a Cape Town travel question, Dirk will answer it personally. If it's a complex itinerary adjustment, we'll update your guide. No question is too small — getting your trip right is the whole point.
