Hi, I'm Dirk

Not a travel writer.
A local who loves visitors.

I moved to Cape Town and never really left. I've hiked Lion's Head in the dark to catch sunrise. I've ridden the Cape Argus. I know the waterfall gorge you can only reach on foot, and the wine estate where the winemaker still pours himself.

Most travel guides tell you the same 10 things. I tell you what I tell my friends — the hidden beach, the R30 train that shows you whales, the sunset spot where you'll have the whole city to yourself.

I built The Cape Town Guide because I was tired of watching visitors miss the best parts of a city I love deeply. This is my attempt to fix that.

— Dirk Zeevenhooven, Cape Town
